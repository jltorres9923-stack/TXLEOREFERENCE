TX LEO · Texas Law Enforcement Reference

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 3, 2026  ·  Developer: Joshua Torres

§ 1Overview

TX LEO is a field reference application designed for Texas law enforcement officers. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

In short: TX LEO collects no personal data. Everything stays on your device.

§ 2Data We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Favorites: Penal code sections and references you save are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage (UserDefaults / local files). This data never leaves your device.
  • Subscription Status: We use Apple's StoreKit framework to process subscriptions. We do not collect or store your payment information. All billing is handled entirely by Apple.

Data Collected Automatically

  • Crash Reports: If enabled in your iOS settings, Apple may share anonymized crash reports with us through Xcode Organizer. These contain no personally identifiable information.
  • App Analytics: If you have opted into sharing analytics with Apple, Apple may share aggregated, anonymized usage statistics with us (e.g., how often the app is opened). We cannot identify individual users from this data.

§ 3Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information.
  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not collect biometric data.
  • We do not collect search queries or browsing behavior.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude).
  • We do not sell or share your data with any third party.

§ 4Subscriptions

TX LEO offers an auto-renewing subscription managed entirely through Apple's App Store. All purchases, billing, and subscription management are handled by Apple. We never see or store your payment information. To manage or cancel your subscription, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone.

§ 5Data Storage

All reference content (penal codes, traffic codes, case law) is bundled directly with the app and stored on your device. No reference data is transmitted to external servers.

§ 6Children's Privacy

TX LEO is intended for use by adult law enforcement professionals. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

§ 7Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
